Balsamic-Glazed Pork Tenderloin with Roasted Carrots & Green Bean

Ingredients:

For the pork:
	•	1 pork tenderloin (about ¾ to 1 lb)
	•	2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
	•	1 tbsp olive oil
	•	1 tsp Dijon mustard 
	•	2 cloves garlic, minced
	•	½ tsp salt
	•	¼ tsp black pepper

⸻

Instructions:

1. Marinate the Pork:
	•	Mix balsamic vinegar, olive oil, garlic, mustard, salt, and pepper.
	•	Coat the pork and marinate for 15–30 minutes (or up to overnight).

2. Roast the Pork:
	•	Preheat oven to 400°F.
	•	Sear pork in a hot oven-safe skillet (or pan) for 2–3 minutes per side until browned.
	•	Transfer skillet to the oven and roast for 15–20 minutes, or until internal temp reaches 145°F.
	•	Rest 5 minutes before slicing.


4. Serve:
	•	Slice pork and serve. Drizzle with extra balsamic.

⸻

Total Time:

~40 minutes (including rest/marinate overlap)
