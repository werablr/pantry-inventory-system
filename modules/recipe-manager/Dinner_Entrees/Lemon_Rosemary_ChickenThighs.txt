# Monday Dinner: Sheet Pan Lemon Rosemary Chicken Thighs with Brussels Sprouts and Potatoes

## Recipe Overview
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes  
**Cook Time:** 30-35 minutes  
**Total Time:** 45-50 minutes  
**Serves:** 2 people  
**Calories:** ~476 per serving  


## Ingredients

### For the Marinade:
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- Juice and zest of 1 large lemon
- 2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, chopped (or 1 tablespoon dried)
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

### Main Ingredients:
- 4-6 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (about 2 lbs)
- 1 lb Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
- 1 lb baby potatoes, quartered (or fingerling potatoes)
- 1 lemon, sliced into rounds
- 2-3 fresh rosemary sprigs for garnish

## Instructions

### Step 1: Prepare (5 minutes)
1. Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C)
2. Line a large rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper
3. Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels

### Step 2: Make Marinade (5 minutes)
1. In a large bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice and zest, chopped rosemary, minced garlic, salt, and pepper
2. Reserve about 1/3 of marinade for vegetables

### Step 3: Marinate Chicken (5 minutes minimum)
1. Place chicken thighs in remaining marinade
2. Toss to coat well
3. Let marinate for at least 5 minutes (can marinate overnight for deeper flavor)

### Step 4: Prepare Vegetables (5 minutes)
1. Add Brussels sprouts and quartered potatoes to reserved marinade
2. Toss until well coated
3. Arrange vegetables evenly on prepared sheet pan

### Step 5: Arrange and Bake (30-35 minutes)
1. Nestle marinated chicken thighs on top of vegetables, skin-side up
2. Scatter lemon slices around the pan
3. Place fresh rosemary sprigs over everything
4. Bake for 30-35 minutes until:
   - Chicken internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C)
   - Chicken skin is golden brown and crispy
   - Brussels sprouts are caramelized on edges
   - Potatoes are tender when pierced with fork

### Step 6: Finish and Serve
1. Let rest for 5 minutes
2. Squeeze roasted lemon slices over everything
3. Garnish with fresh rosemary
4. Season with additional salt and pepper if desired

## Cooking Tips
- **For Extra Crispy Skin:** Broil for 1-2 minutes at the end
- **Make Ahead:** Can marinate overnight for deeper flavors
- **Doneness Check:** Use meat thermometer for 165°F internal temperature
- **Vegetable Substitutions:** Can use broccoli or butternut squash instead of Brussels sprouts
